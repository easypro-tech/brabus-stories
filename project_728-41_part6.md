**Project 728-41**
**Part VI - Sync**

---

The line should not have existed.

I checked the input log.
No command was issued.

The keyboard is inactive.

Yet the line exists.

---

I reread the entry.

It describes an action occurring now.

Match - exact.

Causality - undetermined.

---

The viewport is cold beneath my fingers.

Below - a blue planet.
Third from a yellow dwarf.

I have been observing it for two and a half million orbits.

---

My age - four billion Earth years.
My Homeworld - a billion light-years from here.

In my skull - quantum-neural nodes.

Every nanosecond should be recorded.
Any frame instantly accessible.

---

I remember everything.

No.

Not everything.

---

Gaps have appeared in the archive.

Some entries have vanished.

Some appear.

Sometimes an entry emerges before the event.

---

Self-analysis protocol active.
System stable.
Deviations - acceptable.

I have been repeating this for six hours.

The ship is silent.

It is always silent.
An ideal environment for observation.

No scents.
No wind.
No temperature.

Only data.

---

I open the archive.
Close the archive.
Open it again.

Entry:

728-41 / Earth / observation continues

---

I scroll through the archive.

The old man at the force field.
The monk with bread.
The man in the submarine.
The button beneath my hand.

---

On Earth, it is night.

A man sits before a laptop.

The screen illuminates the desk.
A mug of cold tea.
An ashtray.
Cables.

The laptop fan hums softly.

Beyond the window - a dark courtyard.

---

He opens a file.
Closes the file.
Opens it again.

Filename:

project_728-41_part6

---

I increase observation resolution.

Until this moment, I watched the species.
The civilization.
The statistics.

Now - one.

---

The signal is unstable.

I register noise.

Not atmospheric.
Not electrical.

Different.

It resembles fatigue.

---

The man stands.

Walks to the window.

Opens it.

Cold air enters the room.

He stands there for several seconds.

Closes the window.

Returns.

---

Sits down.

Opens the text.

Types.

---

I read.

Text appears on the screen faster than I can analyze.

> The observer on the ship does not sleep.
> He watches the screen.

Match - exact.

---

The man continues writing.

> He should not be watching one person.
> But he watches.

---

The system suggests correction.

NORMAL.

I ignore it.

---

The man types a new line.

> He watches.

---

He raises his hand.

Finger above the Delete key.

Pause.

The finger does not move.

---

I analyze the gesture.

The command is not executed.

Not due to the system.
Not due to malfunction.

The command simply does not occur.

---

The man lowers his hand.

The line remains.

Whole.

---

He stands.

Steps away from the laptop.

The screen stays on.

---

After some time, he returns.

Sits down.

Looks at the screen.

The text has grown longer.

He did not type.

He simply reads.

He rereads the line.

Then again.

Then stops trying to understand.

New lines appear on the screen.

---

I read them with him.

We read simultaneously.

---

I watch the screen.

The man watches the screen.

The text continues.
